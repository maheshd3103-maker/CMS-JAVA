üè¶ Account Related
How do I check my account balance? +
Login to your account and navigate to the "Account Information" section in your dashboard. Your current balance will be displayed there.
How do I update my account details? +
Currently, account details can be updated by contacting our support team through the complaint system. Select "Account Issue" as the complaint type.
üìù Complaint Related
How do I raise a complaint? +
Login to your account, click on "Raise a Complaint" from the sidebar menu, fill in the complaint form with type, description, and issue date, then submit. You'll receive a tracking ID.
How do I track my complaint? +
Go to "Track the Complaint" section, enter your tracking ID (e.g., CMS1234567890), and click "Track Complaint". You'll see the current status and details.
What are the different complaint statuses? +
Pending: Your complaint has been received and is awaiting review.
In Progress: Our team is actively working on resolving your issue.
Resolved: Your complaint has been successfully resolved.
How long does it take to resolve a complaint? +
Resolution time varies by complaint type. Typically, billing issues take 24-48 hours, technical issues 24 hours, and service issues 24-72 hours. You'll receive updates as we progress.
üí≥ Billing Related
I see incorrect charges on my account. What should I do? +
Raise a complaint with type "Billing Issue" and provide details of the incorrect charges. Our billing team will investigate and provide a detailed breakdown within 24-48 hours.
üîß Technical Issues
I can't login to my account. What should I do? +
First, ensure you're using the correct email and password. Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. If the issue persists, raise a complaint with type "Technical Issue".
The website is not loading properly. What can I do? +
Try refreshing the page, clearing browser cache, or using a different browser. If the problem continues, raise a technical complaint with details about the issue.
üìû Contact & Support
How can I contact customer support? +
The best way to reach us is through the complaint system. Raise a complaint with your issue, and our team will respond promptly. You can track the progress using your tracking ID.
